About TeamExplorer

Team explorer is an application that allows you to work with people around the world. In this way, you can learn from them and they from you. Create your own team or go in another. Gain experience. That's what the IT system is all about.

How does it work?

Create an account, describe what you're looking for and what programming languages you like, and find the best team for you! In this way you can learn how to work within a team of people that you don't know so you can improve your social skills and gain experience at the same time.
